About

Parking space has been a growing problem in the last years in the crowded Baia Mare, with more and more people opting for individual transportation to work, school and for leisure activities.

ParkPoint has the goal to alleviate this problem by offering you the option to inform yourself, save time and even money you would otherwise pay on parking fines!

See statistics about traffic in Baia Mare

Use ParkPoint to read statistics depicting the busiest parking places in Baia Mare. These statistics are used for a more informed decision of the planning and construction of new parking lots based on real-time data. ParkPoint saves information such as the frequence of each parking lot's occupation as well as the time intervals in order to deliver accurate statistics.

Find parking places in Baia Mare

Use ParkPoint when you want to find the Parking Places available in your area. The buffer tool allows you to point at any area you want on the map and find parking places in your desired radius. ParkPoint makes sure you receive the most up-to-date details about the prices and availability of any parking lot in Baia Mare thanks to the sensors mounted in the parking lots in the city which transmit information to this platform 24/7 so you can have a better experience on-the-go.
